Metric Guide:

  • Photo Completeness - Are there enough photos to show the item from multiple angles?
  • Photo Authenticity - Do the images appear to be real photos of the item, not stock or copied?
  • Condition Clarity - Is the item's condition clearly and accurately shown?
  • Description Detail - Does the listing include sufficient specific information?
  • Description Reliability - Does the description feel honest and free from contradictions?
  • Price Realism - Is the price reasonable for this type of item?
  • Price Justification - Does the condition and description support the price?
  • Seller Transparency - Does the seller provide clear and trustworthy information about themselves?
  • Seller Behaviour - Does the seller act normally and without pressure or suspicious behaviour?
  • Risk of Scam - Overall likelihood that the listing is a scam

Scoring:

  • 10 = Strong positive signal
  • 1 = Strong negative signal
  • Unknown = Not enough information yet
  • A score will only be assigned when there is clear evidence.
